My Teaching Philosophy

"As an educator, I aspire to be a facilitator of growth, a source of inspiration and a mentor who empowers students to reach their full potential. My teaching philosophy is grounded in the belief that education is not just about transferring knowledge, but also about fostering personal and intellectual growth, critical thinking and a lifelong love for learning."

Hayley Creed, 2024
